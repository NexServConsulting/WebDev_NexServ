Process & Timeline

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope:

  • Simple website (5-10 pages): 4-6 weeks
  • Medium website (10-25 pages): 6-10 weeks
  • Complex website (25+ pages, custom features): 10-16 weeks
  • E-commerce platform: 8-14 weeks

These timelines include design, development, testing, and revisions. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.

Our design process follows these key phases:

  1. Discovery (1 week): Understanding your goals, audience, and requirements
  2. Research (1 week): Market analysis and competitor research
  3. Wireframing (1-2 weeks): Creating structural layouts and user flow
  4. Design (2-3 weeks): High-fidelity visual designs and mockups
  5. Development (3-8 weeks): Building the website functionality
  6. Testing (1 week): Quality assurance and user testing
  7. Launch (1 week): Deployment and final optimizations

Our packages include different revision rounds:

  • Starter Package: 2 rounds of revisions
  • Growth Package: 3 rounds of revisions
  • Enterprise Package: Unlimited revisions

Additional revisions can be purchased for $500 per round. We also offer ongoing retainer services for continuous optimization.

Pricing & Payment

Our pricing varies based on project scope and complexity:

  • Basic Website: $5,000 - $8,000
  • Professional Website: $8,000 - $15,000
  • E-commerce Platform: $12,000 - $25,000
  • Custom Web Application: $20,000 - $50,000+
  • Brand Identity Package: $5,000 - $12,000

Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific requirements.

Our standard payment structure is:

  • 50% deposit to begin the project
  • 30% payment at design approval
  • 20% final payment upon project completion

We accept wire transfers, credit cards, and ACH payments. For projects over $25,000, we can discuss milestone-based payment schedules.

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for qualified clients:

  • 3-month payment plan: Available for projects under $15,000
  • 6-month payment plan: Available for projects $15,000 - $30,000
  • 12-month payment plan: Available for projects over $30,000

Payment plans require a 25% deposit and include a small financing fee. Contact us to discuss payment plan options.

No hidden costs! Our quotes include all specified deliverables. However, additional costs may apply for:

  • Stock photography or premium fonts (if not provided)
  • Third-party software licenses or subscriptions
  • Website hosting and domain registration
  • Ongoing maintenance and support packages
  • Additional features requested after project scope is finalized

All potential additional costs are discussed upfront and approved by you before any work begins.
